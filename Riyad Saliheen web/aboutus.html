About Us

Riyad Saliheen International School (RSIS)

Situated at House 9, Road D, Saint Lucy Estate, Olambe, Ogun State, Riyad Saliheen International School is a school where staff and students work together, striving towards a common goal. RSIS is a second home, a home away from home where future leaders are nutured.

We imbibe in our students the ABCs of positive values ranging from appreciation to boldness, to cooperation and to dedication, to mention a few. Our students live every school day as agile and enthusiastic youths fuelled with the zeal to carry on with determination. Mentored by a team of dedicated staff, they relentlessly soar in their achievements.

Our staff, both teaching and non-teaching are ever caring and welcoming. They enjoy doing what they know how to do, confidently and exceptionally. That is why our students are so lucky to have such a bunch of dedicated individuals, who work as a team with the spirit of selflessness and sacrifice.
