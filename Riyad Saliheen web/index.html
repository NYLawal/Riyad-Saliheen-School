Welcome to Riyad Saliheen

A second home, where future leaders are nutured

This is Riyad Saliheen International School, a private, islamic school established to develop kids and teenagers into future adults who will uphold positive values in the society and change the world into a safe and enjoyable place to live.

Founded in 1996, Riyad Saliheen International School is a preparatory to senior secondary three, day and boarding school for boys and girls in and around Ogun state. RIYAD has been a school of choice for familes who value academic excellence alongside religious and moral excellence.

Whether in the classroom, the domitory, the mosque or in the community, every minute of every school day at RIYAD helps kids to grow into disiplined and upright youths.

  • Our Vision

    To build creative and resourceful youths who will meet world standards in their academics as well as the utmost level of uprightness in morals and religion.

  • Our Mission

    We aim to produce all-round balanced and independent individuals through the provision of

    • Sound and condusive environment devoid of distractions
    • Committed and talented teachers who give their best to empower their students with the right knowledge and skills
    • Relevant tools and competencies that enable our students to stand their ground without fear of intimidation academically or socially
    • The creation of healthy competition and friendliness among our students, which will assist them in actualising their potentials and developing creative minds

  • Our Standards

    • M- Moral
